June 20, 2013 Meetup

The June Orlando Tech Meetup was held at Voxeo, who graciously provided their awesome space.

Two companies presented:

Daniel Sosa and Ryan McCabe demonstrated Ocoos, an application for hosting complete web sites that include scheduling and payments for small service businesses.

Cameron Cress demonstrated TeeAgent, an application for getting deals on tee times. They explained their business model and went into some great detail about how they don't cannibalize the sales of their golf course clients.

Robb Schiller demonstrated this new GitHub backed web site for the Orlando Tech community.

Beer was provided by Keith Kanouse of The Tech Law Firm and Atlantic.net and TriNet sponsored.
